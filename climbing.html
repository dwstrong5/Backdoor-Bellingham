Vital Rock Climbing Gym


Vital Rock Climbing Gym offers an indoor assortment of climbing fixtures of varying difficulty. Coupled with a welcoming environment of fellow climbers, it's the perfect spot to get some climbing in if the weather is poor or if you just prefer to climb indoors! Membership is required.

Major Cities near Bellingham


Clayton Beach is one of the most popular bouldering spots in Bellingham. A flexible set of problems along the beach and coastline yields great views, good holds, and overhangs. Depending on the tide, you can explore northern and southern ends of the beach and find interesting traverses, with an occasional overhang over shallow water.







WWU Student?


Get started climbing at the Wade King Recreation Center on WWU campus with a host of avid climbers willing to show you the ropes. Western also offers a variety of classes and one-on-one instruction with members of the WWU Climbing team to help all climbers reach new heights! Gear rental availabile.
