Around the Bellingham area there are several locations to hike at. Some of these trails allow you to camp overnight at a designated spot.

    • About an hour from campus and makes for incredible trips. Where most backpacking near bellingham would be.
  • Olympic National Park
    • Out on the Olympic Penisula.
  • Other Backpacking locations
